About FoodShare

Our mission & vision

FoodShare is a platform dedicated to reducing food waste and fighting hunger in Solapur District.

We connect food donors — restaurants, hotels, and individuals — with NGOs and volunteers who distribute food to those in need.

Our goal is to ensure no food goes to waste while helping feed hungry people across all 11 talukas of Solapur.
